Founder’s vision

Our ambition is clear: to be your trusted partner in transforming and digitizing IT and business services. Our expertise is built on a dedicated team of ServiceNow experts and a proven, industrialized methodology, successfully applied with major clients such as ENGIE, SCOR, BNP Paribas, EUROMASTER, AND MORE. Through our Center of Excellence and Innovation, we heavily invest in innovation, developing new use cases to meet the emerging needs of our clients. Together, we co-create ServiceNow portals that deliver optimal user experiences. We firmly believe that our business model ensures the successful adoption of the ServiceNow platform and drives a positive impact on your business objectives, while creating meaningful social and economic impact

Inclusion and Diversity

At UX4MATION, diversity is not a box to check—it’s integral to who we are. We take deliberate actions to foster inclusion at every level, and our Great Place to Work (GPTW) certification is a testament to our dedication to employee well-being and engagement.

Our team spans across the globe, with members from France, Morocco, Tunisia, India, and beyond. We believe that our diverse cultures enrich the UX4MATION experience, creating an environment where learning, sharing, and growth thrive through different perspectives.

We envision a UX4MATION world where every unique trait, personality and mindset is a stepping stone toward collective growth and success.
